## 4. How To Enter
1. Register at xprize.devpost.com ("Join Hackathon") with a Devpost account.
2. Get access to required developer tools/platform; build a Project. Free credits for Google AI Ultra & Google Cloud.
3. Complete all required fields on "Enter a Submission" during the Submission Period.

### Project Requirements
- **What to create:** a business that operates with AI, within ≥1 Category:
  Education & Human Potential · Entrepreneurship & Job Creation · Small Business Services ·
  Money & Financial Access · Professional Services.
- **Functionality:** demonstrate/explain how AI transforms workflows (in video and/or text).
- **Platforms:** must use ≥1 product from **Google Cloud**; must run on its intended platform.
- **New Projects Only:** created after the start of the Submission Period. If pre-existing templates/
  frameworks/boilerplates/snippets were used, **must explain** how the Project utilized them.
- **Third-Party Integrations:** must be authorized per their terms/licenses.
- **LLM Model Use:** projects with LLM functionality must use the **Gemini API for ≥1 LLM call in the
  deployed application** (other LLMs allowed alongside).

### Submission Requirements
- Project meeting the above + Category selection.
- **Code repository URL** (all source). Public (with licensing) **or** private shared with
  `testing@devpost.com` **and** `judging@hacker.fund`.
- **Text description** of how the Project meets requirements + Category relevance.
- **Demo video:** <3 min, shows the Project working on its device, public on YouTube/Vimeo/Youku,
  link on the form, no third-party trademarks/copyrighted music without permission.
- **Revenue evidence:**
  - Total Revenue (arms-length third-party customers, in USD).
  - Revenue by Month: May, June, July, August 2026.
  - Total Costs (excluding marketing/customer acquisition) + one-sentence description.
  - Marketing and Customer Acquisition Spend (disclose even if zero).
- **User evidence:** number of users + high-level breakdown of who they are + testimonials/feedback
  (users must be aware their info is shared).
- **Related-Party Revenue:** from team/family/related entities/pre-existing relationships, reported separately.
- **Evidence of product running:** agent execution logs, API usage records, dashboard screenshots —
  anything showing playbooks running continuously in production.
- If an organization: include **corporate ID**.
- During review, may be asked for more evidence: customer contact info, live demo, additional financials.

### Multiple Submissions
Allowed, but each must be unique and substantially different.

### Testing
Must provide free, unrestricted access to a working Project (credentials if private) until the Judging Period ends.

### Language
All materials in **English** or with an **English translation** (video, text description, testing instructions, etc.).

### Intellectual Property
Submission must be original, solely owned, not violating others' rights.

### Financial or Preferential Support
Must not be developed with financial/preferential support from Sponsor or Administrator.

## 5. Submission Modifications
Drafts allowed before close. After the Submission Period ends, **no changes** (but you can keep updating the
Project in your Devpost portfolio). Sponsor/Devpost may allow limited post-period edits only to remove
infringing/PII/inappropriate material.

## 6. Judges & Criteria
- **Stage One (pass/fail):** baseline viability — fits theme + reasonably applies required APIs/SDKs.
- **Stage Two (equally weighted):**
  1. **Business Viability** — launch a real business, acquire real users, generate real revenue in 90 days.
  2. **AI-Native Operations** — run the business through AI; AI live in production executing key decisions.
  3. **Category Impact** — meaningfully move the needle in the chosen category.
- Verification: may require live call + additional financial docs + proof of user relationships.
  **Email requests must be answered within 2 business days.**
- Tie-breaking by highest score on the first applicable criterion, etc.

## 7. Intellectual Property Rights

(name/likeness/voice/image) during the Hackathon Period and 3 years after.
